Let's Join Forces for betterment of academics

In today's complex academic environment, students face new challenges that can impact their success. Our online tutoring site provides personalized support from experienced tutors to help students improve their grades, build confidence, and develop essential skills for school and beyond.

What We Offer

Insta Connect

If you're uncertain and require prompt assistance, don't worry, as we provide InstaConnect. You can reach out to our skilled tutor at any time for guidance. Our tutor will assist you in learning the problem-solving method on our user-friendly whiteboard and chat-based tutoring platform.

Learn more

Q Drop

If you're looking to avoid long wait times for assistance, you can simply submit your question in our "QDrop" feature. Our experienced tutor will review it and offer you a solution and problem-solving technique. You can expect to receive an answer to your question within six hours.

Learn more

Schedule Smart

Would you like to arrange a tutoring session that fits your schedule? You can utilize ScheduleSmart, which allows you to schedule a session at your convenience. Our tutor will be available at the designated time to assist you. However, please remember to attend the scheduled sessions on time!

Learn more

Personalized learning

We are providing 1:1 Tutoring services where our tutors can assist the student with personalized learning opportunities for students who may struggle with certain subjects or need extra help in any concept, resulting in a more individualized educational experience.

Increased student engagement

Our online tutoring platform has been equipped with interactive tools such as audio, video ,chat and virtual whiteboard to collaborate the student with the tutors and work on the problem real time. This makes online tutoring a more engaging learning experience for students.

Customization

Our services can be customized to meet the needs of our clients.

Methodology

Our methodology is prepared by taking the school's academic requirements, allowing our tutors to adjust as per the approach of the school's teaching method. We are committed to helping students achieve their academic goals within the boundaries set by their school.

Time Duration

Our tutoring sessions typically range from 10 to 60 minutes, but we can modify the sessions to align with the school's requirements

Learning Objective

We can modify our methodology by considering the learning objective of the school and make the necessary modifications to our tutoring process to ensure that students can achieve their academic goals.

Curriculum

Our tutors are trained based on the school's curriculum to ensure that they are fully prepared to provide the best possible support to students.
