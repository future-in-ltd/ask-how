7 Reasons Why Online Learning is Best

Despite the COVID pandemic playing a major part in the exponential rise of the virtual classroom phenomenon. We cannot deny the fact that edtech has always had the potential to boom beyond limits.

As a consequence, when the Coronavirus crippled the education system all over the world, putting the future of students at risk, online tutoring emerged as a life saviour for students, teachers, and parents placing their faith in the online tutoring system.

Therefore, there is no doubt that online tutoring will stay for a long time in our lives and we will examine some of the key advantages that students, teachers, and parents can reap from online learning in this article.

#1 Bridges the gap

Due to the pandemic, every student has to face some challenges in their path of education. Thus online learning helps students to overcome these challenges and bridge the gap which has occurred in their learning process. Whether you have missed a class or didn't understand something in school, online learning can solve every problem that occurs.

#2 Convenient

Online learning provides us so many powers which include learning at the time we want to by scheduling a session, along with this we can choose a tutor as well to get guided by. And on top of all this, we are free to choose our location and environment as well in which we want to learn. This provides more time to learn and eliminates the time of travelling to classrooms.

#3 Encourages students

Learning from a platform with a personalised schedule and tutor automatically encourages a student to learn and excel. It is believed that when we are learning in an environment where we can learn according to our capacity and choice we will learn faster. As online learning platforms provide students with a lot of choices to choose from, a student is motivated to learn faster.

#4 Customised Learning

Online tutoring provides students with tailored or customised learning methods, schedules and tutors. Students don't have to wait or cope with other students in the classroom, instead they learn at the time and speed they are comfortable with.

#5 Enhanced grades

Online learning helps students to perform better and enhance their grades through one-on-one interaction with their tutors. Through this tutors can closely monitor their strengths and weaknesses and can help them overcome any obstacle.

#6 Affordable

Learning at private tuition can be costly but learning through online platforms are cost-effective as the tutors are available to guide you at low prices. Along with this, you can also save the money you spent on travelling to tuition centres as well.

#7 Interactive

There are some students who are shy or who feel uncomfortable while asking questions in the classroom. So to overcome this problem online learning has introduced personal sessions with the tutors in which students can ask their queries without feeling uncomfortable and can interact more and automatically will learn more.
