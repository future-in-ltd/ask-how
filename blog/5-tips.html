5 TIPS TO IMPROVE YOUR MEMORY POWER

Memory is the basic process that we use to acquire, store, and later retain information. The importance of our memory and concentration level is at the peak during our school phase. Most of the learning and understanding depends on the fact that how much you can retain and retrieve that information. The biggest drawback that the students face in their educational process is how they are unable to concentrate in class, and thus, end up having a poor memory.

The basis of a good academic performance is formed by your day-to-day concentration in the classroom and your ability to remember what you have read. Therefore, we bring to you five such tested tips to increase your memory power and make you a smart learner.

1. INVOLVE YOUR SENSES

Always remember that learning is a dynamic process. This means that you are continuously learning and remembering things through all your senses. You learn when you watch, you learn when you hear, and you learn when you touch. In fact, the more you will involve your senses in your learning process, the better you will retrieve the information. So begin to expand your horizons of learning and involve all your senses in it. Read the materials. Remember them through their colors or pictures, and link them with the shows or movies you watch. This makes the information stay longer in your brain because you have attached it to all your senses.

2. PRACTICE, PRACTICE!

It is a known fact that practicing something again and again makes your brain familiar with it. Whatever you learn in the classroom, make it a habit to keep revising it over and over. Do not wait for the exam days to study. Study and go through your notes every day. Write them down or read them out loud, but keep repeating them unless your brain becomes familiar with the information. You can do this by making a study schedule and adhering to it strictly.

3. CHANGE YOUR LEARNING PATTERN

Start including more learning techniques such as using acronyms, mnemonics, or chunks while studying. Break down your lessons into parts and study them in small amounts. Don’t overburden your brain with too much information. Track your learning pattern and see at what time you are able to concentrate the most- day time or night time. You can also try to visualize what you are studying. This particularly helps people who have a better visual learning style. Carefully analyzing your study patterns and combining it with other techniques can help you in increasing your memory.

4. HAVE A HEALTHY LIFESTYLE

Studying is very important but it should not make you compromise other things in your life, because then you will lose the equilibrium and won’t be able to benefit from anything. So, to improve your memory and concentration, make sure you are living a balanced life. This means take proper sleep, eat a healthy diet, and exercising two to three days in a week. When you follow a good and stress-free lifestyle, you are more at peace, and a person who is more peaceful can also concentrate and remember better.

5. MEDITATION

Over the years, meditation has received a lot of attention from people for being beneficial for their mental health. However, the benefits of meditation do not just stay rooted to mental health. A lesser-known fact is that practicing meditation daily also increases the blood flow to your brain, which boosts the blood vessels in the cerebral cortex- the area that is responsible for dealing with tasks such as memory and concentration. So, including a minimum of five minutes of meditation in your day-to-day schedule can really help you in boosting your memory power. Apart from these study tips, some other ways through which you can improve your memory are as follows:

  • Learn to manage your stress
  • Take regular study breaks
  • Relate new information to things you already know.
  • Solve crossword puzzles and related memory games
  • Structure and organize your schedule
  • Read out loud

CONCLUSION

CONCLUSION A strong memory is a very essential kit for success. As we grow older, our memories begin to fade. So, it is very important to keep involved in activities that strengthen our memory power. Follow these tips earnestly, on a regular basis, and in no time you will be able to better focus and remember more. All you need is dedication and the will to change. If you have that, nothing can stop you from achieving what you truly want.
